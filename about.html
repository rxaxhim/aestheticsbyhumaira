Meet Dr. Humaira

Dr. Humaira, a certified laser and advanced aesthetics specialist, brings over a decade of hands-on experience and a deep passion for skincare. Originally trained in dermatology, she combines medical expertise with a personalized, compassionate approach that puts client comfort and safety first.

Whether you're looking to refresh your appearance or invest in long-term skin health, you'll experience top-tier care in a warm, welcoming clinic environment. Discover why so many clients trust Dr. Humaira for the best in laser aesthetics and skincare in Mississauga.
